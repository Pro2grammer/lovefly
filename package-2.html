Honeymoon / Couple Special

Kashmir Deluxe 5N/6D tour package

Join Love Fly Travels for memorable holiday experience.
Enjoy the tour of heaven in just 6 days including Shikara ride in Dal Lake, experiencing night stay in houseboat and sunrise from the gallery of houseboat. We let you enjoy the Mughal Gardens of Srinagar like Nishat Garden, Shalimar Garden, Pari Mahal, Cheshmashi, Hazratbal Shrine and Shankara Charya Temple. Visiting Gulmarg awaits with its snow-covered mountains, having Gondola ride from bottom of the hill to the top of Affarwat hill and creating the memories by visiting to Pahalgam. Capture the memories of Romantic Candle Light Dinner. Embark on a captivating journey with us as we guide you through renowned destinations and taking in the stunning scenery.

Tour Plan Itinerary:

DAY 1: Arrival at Srinagar

The first day of the Kashmir Package begins with a warm welcome at the airport by our representative who will hold a placard with your name on it. You will then be transferred to the 3-star deluxe hotel where you can refresh yourself. In the afternoon, you will embark on a tour of some of the most beautiful places in Kashmir, starting with the famous Dal Lake and enjoy a romantic Shikara ride included in package. You will then visit the Cheshmashahi (Royal Spring), Pari Mahal (Palace of Fairies), Nishat Garden (garden of bliss), Shalimar Garden (Abode of joy). Each of these destinations offers a unique glimpse into the rich culture and history of Kashmir, and the stunning scenery is sure to leave a lasting impression. After a full day of sightseeing, return to the hotel for a delicious dinner, making for a memorable first day in Kashmir.

Day 2: Srinagar to Sonamarg (90 kms)

After delicious breakfast, start the day tour to Sonamarg via Gagangir. This drive of 90 Kms from Srinagar to Sonamarg is very beautiful as you will pass through some beautiful towns and villages, at many places you can enjoy the view of paddy fields with flowing Sindh River from one side. In Sonamarg, you can visit Thajiwas Glacier which is 4 Kms from Sonamarg, a local taxi can be hired. (Also you can visit Baltal at your own cost). Sonamarg is also called Meadows of Gold. Return to the Srinagar , have a dinner and overnight stay at Srinagar hotel.

Day 3: Srinagar to Gulmarg (52 Kms)

The third day starts with a delicious breakfast to energize you for a day .Today, you will visit the majestic hill station of Gulmarg(meadows of flowers), surrounded by top Himalayan peaks. During the journey, you will have the opportunity to admire the scenic apple orchards. Once you arrive in Gulmarg, you will visit several interesting places, including the Golf Course, Rani Temple, Heritage Church, and can have a Gondola ride, Horse ride, Helicopter ride or Bike ride. You can use the ski- lift for skiing/snowboarding from hill tops. After an action-packed day of exploring, take a rest , having a delicious dinner and Stay a night at hotel in Gulmarg.

Day 4: Gulmarg to Pahalgam (150 Kms)

Morning after breakfast, check out from the hotel and we will begin our trip to Pahalgam (The Valley of Shepherds) at an altitude of 2200 mts. The total distance of 150 Kms will be covered in 4hrs. En-route visit the saffron field of Pampore and the cricket bat factory where one of the world famous cricket bats are made of willow tree by hand. Also visit the ancient temple of Avantipura ruins which is an eleven hundred years old temple & mattan temple. Enjoy the countryside drive through the pine forest which is the most famous place for the Indian Film Industry. Arrive and check in at the hotel after fresh up. Explore the streets of Pahalgam and you can visit Betaab Valley, Aru Valley and Chandanwari which are famous for natural beauty over there(at your own cost). Evening return back to your hotel, Dinner will be served overnight in the hotel at Pahalgam.

Day 5: Pahalgam to Srinagar (90Kms)

Wake up refreshed and start your day with a breakfast while enjoying the magnificent morning view of Pahalgam. Today, you will indulge in the excitement of pony riding through the mountainous trails surrounded by forests after pony ride you have to check-out and proceed to Srinagar and visit Shankara Charya Temple at top of Zabarwan Hill and explore the markets of Srinagar city. After an exhilarating day, return to the hotel/houseboat for your overnight stay at Srinagar.

Day 6: Departure

After a delicious breakfast with a breathtaking view of City, it's time to pack up and head to the airport. Make sure to arrive at least two hours before your flight departure. Saying goodbye is difficult, but the time has come for you to depart. However, we will stay in touch with you and look forward to meeting you again on future adventures. Leave Kashmir with sweet memories.
